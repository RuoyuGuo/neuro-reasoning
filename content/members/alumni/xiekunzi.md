Hi, I’m Kunzi, a PhD student working in deep learning for medical image analysis.  
My research focuses on **medical image segmentation**, **image registration**, and **microscopy image analysis**, particularly in histopathology and electron microscopy. I am interested in developing robust and efficient models that can reduce annotation requirements, improve generalisation, and support high-resolution biomedical imaging.

My work explores weakly supervised learning, domain-aware regularisation, and transformer-based architectures to enhance segmentation and registration performance under challenging imaging conditions. I aim to design methods that are both computationally practical and clinically meaningful.

Previously, I completed research projects in automated microscopy image analysis, focusing on noise-robust nuclei segmentation and weakly supervised label utilisation.

Some of my research topics are listed below. If you are interested in any of these areas, I would be happy to discuss further.  
My publications and outputs can be found on my **[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=-j6en_oAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN)** and **[GitHub](https://github.com/winnie172026)** pages.
